Biography

I am a PhD student at School of Earth and Space Sciences, Peking University.

Before coming to PKU, I obtained a bachelor degree in Geodesy and Geomatics from School of Geodesy and Geomatics, Wuhan University (Wuhan, China) in 2013.

My research interests lie in Deep learning, Cloud computing and Remote sensing.
